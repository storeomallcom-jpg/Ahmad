Range Math Explained

Floor Price — The break-even limit: (Cost+Ads+Ship) ÷ (1−Tax). Never go below this.

Target Price — Your margin baked in: Total ÷ (1−Tax−Margin).

Premium Price — Target × 1.15 — absorbs returns, chargebacks & surprise costs.
