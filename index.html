How the math works

Floor price — the minimum you can sell at without losing money. Any lower and every sale is a loss.
Formula: (cost + ads + shipping) ÷ (1 − tax rate)

Target price — the price that achieves exactly your margin goal.
Formula: total ÷ (1 − tax rate − margin rate)

Premium price — target × 1.15. A 15% buffer that absorbs chargebacks, returns, and unexpected costs.
