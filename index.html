Range Math Explained

Floor Price — The break-even limit. Derived as (Cost+Ads+Ship) ÷ (1−Tax). You cannot go below this or you lose money on every sale.

Target Price — Your margin baked in: Total ÷ (1−Tax−Margin). Guarantees exactly your desired profit margin.

Premium Price — Target × 1.15. A 15% buffer to absorb refunds, chargebacks & unexpected costs without eroding profit.
