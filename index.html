Range Math Explained

Net Profit P(s) = s·(1−r)·(1−t) − c − a
Where s=price, r=refund rate, t=tax, c=cost, a=ad spend

Floor — Break-even limit (margin → 0): (c+a) ÷ [(1−r)·(1−t)]. Below this you lose money.

Target — Margin baked in: (c+a) ÷ [(1−r)·(1−t) − m]. Achieves exactly your target margin.

Premium — Target × 1.15. Absorbs refunds, chargebacks & unexpected costs with a 15% buffer.
