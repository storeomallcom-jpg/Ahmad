Range Math Explained

Floor Price — The break-even limit. lim(margin→0): (Cost+Ads+Ship) ÷ (1−Tax). You cannot go below this or you lose money.

Recommended Price — Your target margin baked in: Total ÷ (1−Tax−Margin).

Premium Price — Recommended × 1.15 to absorb refunds, chargebacks & unexpected costs.
